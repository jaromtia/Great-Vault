These interrelated documents (policies, procedures, standards, and guidelines) support a GRC program by providing a structured approach to managing the organization's objectives, assessing and mitigating risks, and complying with relevant laws and regulations. But how do governance committees and risk managers know that the organization is operating in alignment with these requirements? All four types of documents directly support compliance because they provide the details needed by an information compliance team to ensure (via measurement and assessment) that all activities are carried out within defined boundaries.
